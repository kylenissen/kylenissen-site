About

Analytical thinking, product design, and AI-assisted development.

Kyle Nissen is an app developer, analytics professional, and AI-focused builder creating practical tools at the intersection of software, data, and decision-making.

With a background in business analytics, research, survey analysis, and AI-assisted development, Kyle focuses on building products that make complex workflows easier to understand and act on. His work blends analytical thinking with product design, turning messy information into clear, usable experiences.

Kyle’s current projects include tools for Magic: The Gathering players, combining collection management, deckbuilding, simulation, and AI-driven evaluation into apps that help players better understand their cards, decks, and decisions.
