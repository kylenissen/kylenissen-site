About Me

I’m Kyle Nissen, a data scientist and app developer who likes building practical tools. My background is in analytics, research, and AI-assisted development, but my main interest is how technology fits into everyday life: how people use it, where it creates friction, and how it can be made clearer and more helpful. I build apps and tools with a focus on usability, transparency, and practical value.

With a background in business analytics, research, survey analysis, and AI-assisted development, I focus on building products that make complex workflows easier to understand and act on. My work blends analytical thinking with product design, turning messy information into clear, usable experiences.

My current projects include tools for Magic: The Gathering players, combining collection management, deckbuilding, simulation, and AI-driven evaluation into apps that help players better understand their cards, decks, and decisions.
