ScryMark

ScryMark is an AI-driven Magic: The Gathering analytics project focused on deck evaluation, simulation, and decision support.

From intuition to testable insight

Where JourneyFX helps users make quick currency calculations, ScryMark is aimed at helping Magic: The Gathering players better understand performance, strategy, and card choices.

It is designed around the idea that AI and simulation can help evaluate decks, compare options, surface weaknesses, and support more informed brewing decisions.

Analytics for better decisions

ScryMark combines Kyle’s background in analytics with his interest in AI-assisted tools, using data-oriented thinking to help players move from intuition alone toward clearer, more testable insights about their decks and gameplay choices.
